About Me

I am a passionate penetration tester with expertise in identifying and exploiting vulnerabilities across web applications, networks, and systems. My approach combines technical skills with strategic thinking to provide comprehensive security assessments.

I specialize in using Burp Suite for capturing and manipulating requests, crafting payloads, and conducting thorough vulnerability assessments. With a strong foundation in DNS, networking fundamentals, and traffic inspection, I help organizations strengthen their security posture.

When I'm not ethically hacking systems, I'm actively learning on platforms like TryHackMe, expanding my knowledge, and preparing for professional penetration testing opportunities.
